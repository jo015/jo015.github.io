Study Guide

  • Be able to find unknown lengths using proportions in scale drawings
  • Be able to classify a quadrilateral based on side lengths, side relationships, and angle measure
  • Be able to identify conditions for unique triangles
  • Be able to find measures of missing angles using ideas such as adjacent angles (sharing a common vertex and a common side but no common interior points), Supplementary Angles (sum of 180¬∞), Complementary Angles (sum of 90¬∞), and vertical angles (angles formed by two intersecting lines the two angles that do not share sides are congruent)
