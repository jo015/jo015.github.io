Study Guide

  • Be able to identify integers and their opposites
  • Be able to convert fractions to decimals and identify terminating and repeating decimals
  • Be able to add integers.
  • Be able to subtract integers
  • Be able to add and subtract rational numbers
  • Be able to multiply integers
  • Be able to divide integers
  • Be able to multiply and divide rational numbers
  • Be able to solve word problems involving rational numbers and integers
